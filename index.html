About Me

I'm currently enrolled at WeCanCodeIT, a five-star full stack web development course. I entered the world of coding when, at my former job as a client services rep, the CEO of the company approached me and told me that I was hilariously overqualified for my current job, and I should look into something more challenging and tailored to my skillset, like coding. I researched bootcamps, had a talk with the head developer at my office, and fell in love with the concept.

I grew up in South Florida to a family of professional musicians, and spent my free time learning music and playing video games. I was particularly drawn to the computer at an early age, and once I had a computer of my own, I immediately began picking apart the code of my favorite video games, as well as the appearance of the operating system.

After I graduate from the course, I plan on looking for a job as a junior developer and moving forward with this exciting new path.
